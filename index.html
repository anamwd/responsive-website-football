SUPPORTER LIASON OFFICER

One of the innovations introduced by the Financial Fair Play regulation is the figure of the Supporter Liaison Officer. The Supporters Liaison Officer (SLO) is the Delegate of a professional football club dealing with supporters. It is a professional figure included in UEFA's Licensing and Financial Fair Play rules in 2010, implemented by the Italian Football Federation (FIGC) in the organizational criteria for obtaining national licenses as a fulfillment for the participation of football clubs in the professional championships from the 2012/2013 season.
